My name is Mary Leong. I am currently a student at CUNY Brooklyn College studying Computer Science. Particularly interested in front end development and UX Design. I use my organization skills to map out and create things that are functional and appealing to the eyes. I hope to gain more experience so that I can create things that make peoples everyday lives easier.

Childhood: Born and raised in Brooklyn, NY. I spent most of my free time helping my parents are their Bakery. There I learned everything front front of house operations to back of house storage and baking. I was mainly responsible for keeping items in stock, but in time I learned how to create different pastries and design cakes. When I was not in the bakery I was usually at Track Practice. I have forged life long relationships and learned how rewarding it is to be a part of a team.
